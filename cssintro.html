On November 19, the first official look at ‘Jumanji 3’ was shared online on its official social media page. The photo showed all of the main 4 cast members: Dwayne Johnson, Jack Black, Kevin Hart, and Karen Gillian, out and about in the modern city streets in their iconic survival gear. The photo was shared with fans all around the world along with the caption, “Look who’s on the loose (eyes emoji) #JumanjiMovie is coming exclusively to theatres Christmas 2026.”
